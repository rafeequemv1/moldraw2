Ask chemistry drawing questions and shape MolDraw features

The MolDraw Community helps students, researchers, educators, and computational chemists discuss chemical structure drawing, Ketcher editing workflows, molecule export, 3D visualization, spectra tools, and requested improvements to the free MolDraw web app.

What can I do here?
Read public discussions, post workflow questions, reply to threads, upload images, and upvote ideas after signing in.
How are feature requests handled?
Users can upvote requests. Admins can mark requests as new, under review, under progress, or done.
