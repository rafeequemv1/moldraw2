How to Add Lone Pair in MolDraw

This is one of the most frequently asked questions. Use the exact sequence below in the editor.

  1. Select the atom in your structure.
  2. Right-click the selected atom.
  3. Click Edit.
  4. Open the Radical dropdown.
  5. Choose the lone pair option and apply.

Tip: zoom in before selecting small atoms to avoid accidental edits.
