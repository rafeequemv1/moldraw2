Drawing Structures and Bonds

This chapter focuses on clean and accurate 2D molecular drawing. Strong structure quality improves readability and downstream naming/export workflows.

  • Create single, double, triple, and aromatic bonds correctly.
  • Use ring tools and templates to avoid malformed structures.
  • Adjust stereochemistry and atom labels where needed.
  • Use undo/redo and zoom for precision edits.
