Export and File Formats

Choose the right output format based on your destination: manuscript figure, analysis software, or 3D modeling pipeline.

  • Use SVG for publication-quality 2D graphics.
  • Use PNG/JPG for slides and quick sharing.
  • Use SDF/XYZ for chemistry and modeling workflows.
  • Use PDB/OBJ/X3D for structural visualization and 3D tools.
