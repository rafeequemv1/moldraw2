February 2026

Feature Molecule Properties Panel

  • New properties card in the 3D viewer showing compound name, IUPAC name, molecular mass, boiling point, and melting point.
  • Properties are fetched automatically from PubChem when a structure is loaded or drawn.
February 2026

AI Gemini Chat Assistant

  • AI chat assistant in the 3D viewer panel powered by Google Gemini.
  • Ask about IUPAC names, molecular properties, or instruct the AI to draw/edit structures on the canvas.
  • Removed legacy Crisp chat widget.
February 2026

Feature Accumulative PubChem Imports

  • Importing a molecule from PubChem now adds it alongside existing structures on the 2D canvas instead of replacing them.
  • 3D viewer also accumulates imported molecules.
February 2026

Feature IUPAC Name Lookup

  • Added an IUPAC button in the toolbar to look up the IUPAC name of the drawn structure via PubChem.
February 2026

Feature JPEG Export

  • Download 2D structures as JPEG from the toolbar.
  • Export 3D viewer snapshots as JPEG.
February 2026

Feature Molecular Mass Display

  • Shows the molecular mass of the selected molecule in the 3D viewer.
  • Mass updates automatically when drawing or importing a new structure.
February 2026

Feature Protein Controls

  • PDB imports add to the 3D scene without clearing previous models.
  • Toggle button to close/remove a loaded protein from the 3D view.
